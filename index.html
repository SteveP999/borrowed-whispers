About the Band

Hello Texas

Hello Texas makes Red Dirt country music the way Texas intended — loud when it needs to be, quiet when it earns it, and always honest about where it came from.

Equal parts honky tonk swagger and backroad soul, their catalog runs from rowdy anthems to late-night confessions. The kind of band that sounds like the state they're named after.

Signed to Hello Texas Records, the flagship act of the label they share a name with.
